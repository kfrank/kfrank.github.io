Klare Frank

Digital Product Designer

I focus on the holistic design process and have lead design on projects at Vox Media, Treehouse, and ISL.

Desiging at the intersection of form and function

In addition to designing products and websites, I’ve taught Front-End Development at General Assembly and regularly mentor designers looking to add front-end development and designing in-browser to their skillset. I also speak at conferences and have been featured in interviews and podcasts about design and development.

I am currently a part-time masters student in the Human Centered Design and Engineering program at the University of Washington, enhancing my skills in user research and ideation while working new areas such as VR, voice-assisted technology, and concepts for low vision users.

In my free time, I paint abstract acrylic art which have been featured in galleries and on art walks around Seattle. I highly value the balance between work and personal life, and believe having creative projects outside of the the digital world brings greater insight to my work.
