Klare Frank is a self-taught designer and frontend developer. She currently works at Treehouse in Washington, DC creating websites and apps for non-profits, global brands, and Fortune 500 companies. She also teaches frontend development at General Assembly and has spoken at conferences including ContraryCon and Code(Her). When she's not at a computer, Klare enjoys homebrewing, paddleboarding, reading 20th century classic fiction, and biking around the city.

You can find Klare on Twitter and Dribbble or around DC at events like Refresh DC, DC Nightowls, or Women Who Code DC.
