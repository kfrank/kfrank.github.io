Hello there! I’m a self-taught designer and developer based in Seattle, WA, and currently a product designer at Treehouse. Previously, I’ve worked with General Assembly, ISL, and Chief for the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau.

When I’m not at my computer, I enjoy crafting up new homebrew recipes, globetrotting, taking my bikes Tifa and Nell out for rides around town, and creating custom weekly playlists.

You can find me on Twitter and Dribbble. This site was built using Jekyll and is hosted on Github.
