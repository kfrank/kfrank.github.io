Klare is a self-taught designer and frontend developer. She currently works at ISL in Washington, DC creating websites and apps for non-profits, global brands, and Fortune 500 companies. She also teaches frontend development at General Assembly and recently spoke on design process and longevity at ContraryCon.

You can find Klare on Twitter and Dribbble. Additionally, this website's (very much in progress) source can be viewed on Github.
