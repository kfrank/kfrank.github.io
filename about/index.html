Howdy! I'm Klare Frank, a self-taught designer and frontend developer based in Washington, DC. Currently, I'm a designer on the team at Treehouse and am thrilled to be helping people learn the skills to build amazing things. I've also taught frontend development at General Assembly and have spoken at conferences including ContraryCon and Code(Her). When I'm not at a computer, I enjoy crafting up new homebrew recipes, spending a hell of a lot of time in secondhand bookstores looking for the next book to read, taking my mid-90s carbon fiber Trek out for a ride around the city, catching so many shows at the 930 club, or enjoying an old fashioned at my favorite bar.

You can find me on Twitter and Dribbble or around DC at events like Refresh DC, DC Nightowls, or Women Who Code DC.
